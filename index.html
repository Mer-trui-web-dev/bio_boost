ABOUT ME
I am an office administrator, newly exposed to the world of Web Development and Coding.
I started my career as a legal drafter for local government and obtained a diploma in Human Resources Management when I relocated to Canada.
I am honored to be one of Skillhat's Diverse tech Talent Program Scholarship and hope to continue on this journey even after the program comes to an end. Why would a legal / HR professional would want to learn code, you may ask? Because technology is a part of every industry we know. Human Resources technologies are developing in rapid speed and having this knowledge gives me a competitive advantage.
